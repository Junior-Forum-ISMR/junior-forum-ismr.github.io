We invite researchers in machine learning and statistics to participate in the Score-Based Methods Workshop.

Event

This workshop will be an in-person event consisting of two-day seminars held in December 2nd and 3rd, 2022. The seminars will cover invited talks as well as contributed talks.

Key Dates

  • Submission deadline: 22 Sept 2022
  • Acceptance notification: 14 Oct 2022

Submission Details

We invite researchers to submit their recent work on the development, analysis, or application of score-based methods.

A submission should take the form of an extended abstract of 4 pages in PDF format using the template modified from NeurIPS 2022 style.

The review process will be double-blind. Author names need to be anonymized and references may extend as far as needed beyond the 4-page upper limit. Submissions may include a supplement/appendix, but reviewers are not responsible for reading any supplementary material.

Dual submission policy: If a paper has previously appeared in a journal, workshop, or conference (including the NeurIPS 2022 conference), it should be extended in order to be accepted. If a paper is currently under review (e.g. ICLR 2023), it can still be submitted to this workshop.

We will use OpenReview for submission and review.

Questions? Contact us at neurips-2022-workshop-on-score-based-methods@googlegroups.com.
